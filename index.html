Coin Collection

In our navigation app, users earn coins by navigating indoors at Parul University. Coins are awarded for reaching destinations, completing challenges, or passing checkpoints. This encourages users to explore and engage with the app. As they collect coins, users unlock rewards like coupons for university facilities, enhancing their navigation experience.
